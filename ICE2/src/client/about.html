About Animal Manager

Animal Manager is a comprehensive web application designed to help you manage your animal collection. It provides an easy-to-use interface for creating, updating, searching, and managing animal records. Whether you're a pet owner, veterinarian, or animal shelter manager, our system helps you keep track of important animal information like breed, name, physical characteristics, and sounds.

Adding a new animal is simple:
  1. Click the "Create" link in the navigation menu
  2. Fill in the required information (name, breed, number of eyes, number of legs, and sound)
  3. Click the "Create Animal" button
  4. Your animal will be saved and you'll be redirected to the search page
Make sure each animal has a unique name to avoid conflicts.

Yes, you can manage your animals in several ways:
  • Editing: Click the "Edit" button next to any animal in the search page. You can modify all fields except the name.
  • Deleting: Click the "Delete" button next to any animal. A confirmation dialog will appear to prevent accidental deletions.
  • Viewing: Use the search page to see all your animals, with pagination for easy browsing when you have many entries.

Animal Manager uses your browser's local storage to save animal data. This means:
  • Your data remains on your device
  • No internet connection is required after initial page load
  • Data persists between browser sessions
  • Clearing browser data will remove all stored animals
